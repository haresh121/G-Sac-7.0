Workshops

AI/ML and Python Programming

This workshop focuses on introducing the current booming technologies such as AI/ML to the students. Along with an insight into Python Programming and its applications. Various aspects of python programming will be imparted to the students such as Advanced OOPS concepts as well as hands-on exercises using a real-time problem. The students will be provided with certification of merit, a letter of recommendation, a workshop kit and an Internship with Stipend will be provided to the students who clear the written test post workshop.
ORGANIZER: PHOENIX GLOBAL
REGISTRATION FEE: 1500/-
DATES: 19 , 20 December 2019

Cloud Technologies and Docker

During the course of this workshop students will be imparted knowledge about the concepts of virtualization, types of cloud, H/A, DR, evolution of cloud, DBAAS as well as Docker Images, containers and registers, dockerisation of applications and more. As a part if this program the students will be provided a workshop kit, certification of merit as well as Internship with Stipend will be provided to the students who clear the written test post workshop.
REGISTATION FEE: 1000/-
DATES: 20th December, 2019

guest Lectures

Mr. Raj Kumar Bollem
About:

Raj Kumar Bollem, founder of Andhra Pradesh’s one of the largest student organisation, GUSAC is going to talk about alternative opportunities in farming and rural development. Students will be having an opportunity to interact with him throughout the lecture and brainstorm on farming and development of rural areas.

Mr. Leben Johnson
About:

Leben Johnson, who is director of Venture Development Centre (VDC), GUIDE is going to throw some light on entrepreneurship and going to break students’ curiosity on entrepreneurship and start up culture.

Mr. Subhash Kakarla
About:

As a lot of students are confused within the paradoxical options of career after their under-grad course, Subhash Kakarla, chairman- executive board of Phoenix Global is going to expound on career development after engineering and how one can take his career forward with a variety of options

Mr. Guru Charan
About:

Photographer Guru Charan is known for street photography and is one of the thrilling bloggers who believes in capturing with his emotions. Students will get an opportunity to interact with him and know more about street photography
